6. What is the value of each of the following expressions in C89? 
(Give all possible values if an expression may have more than one value.) 
(a) 8 / 5 
(b) -8 / 5 
(c) 8 / -5 
(d) -8 / -5

- a) 1

- b) -1 (round towards 0) or -2 (round towards -∞)

- c) -1 (round towards 0) or -2 (round towards -∞)

- d) 1 (round towards 0) or 2 (round towards +∞)
